Process

How It Works

SiteDesk integrates quickly and runs a consistent commercial rhythm — so variations, claims and correspondence keep moving without you chasing them.

01
Step One
Onboard

We review current project status, contracts, registers and priorities. Then we implement the SiteDesk CA system immediately — structured registers, standardised templates, defined workflows, and key date tracking for Security of Payment Act compliance.

02
Step Two
Manage the Workflow

We manage the weekly commercial workflow: variations, claims, correspondence, action tracking and SOP key dates — every week without fail. Consistent, proactive administration that keeps your project commercially protected.

03
Step Three
Keep You Informed

You receive clear, regular updates and a written snapshot of what is done, what is pending, and what decisions are needed. Transparent. Consistent. Accountable. No surprises.
