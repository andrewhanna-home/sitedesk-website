Frequently Asked

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find what you're looking for? Call us direct.

What's included in "Contract Administration"?+

Variations, claims, subcontract admin, procurement admin, registers, commercial correspondence, and close-out support — set up to keep projects compliant and financially controlled.

Can you jump in mid-project?+

Yes. We can stabilise existing registers, clean up documentation, and establish a weekly rhythm quickly.

Is it locked in?+

No. Subscriptions are month-to-month. Scale support up or down as workload changes.

Is pricing per project or across all?+

Pricing is per month, per active project. If you have multiple projects running concurrently, each is priced individually based on the selected tier.

How quickly can you start?+

We typically onboard and commence within days. Once access to current registers, contracts, and key documents is provided, we begin adding value immediately.

Do you replace an in-house CA?+

We can supplement an existing CA, cover workload gaps, or act as a fully outsourced CA function depending on the tier selected and project requirements.

How do you manage multiple stakeholders?+

We coordinate closely with your site team, consultants, and subcontractors, maintaining clear registers and written communication to ensure accountability and timely decision-making.

What types of projects do you support?+

Residential, mixed-use, and commercial construction projects across Australia, with services tailored to suit project scale and complexity.

Who will be doing the work?+

All contract administration work is led and overseen by the Director of SiteDesk, who has over a decade of experience as a Contract Administrator, Senior CA, and Project Manager across multiple construction sectors.

What is "SiteDesk systems implementation"?+

At the start of each engagement, SiteDesk implements our proven CA software onto your project — structured registers, standardised templates, defined workflows, and key date tracking for Security of Payment Act compliance. No changes to how your site team builds.

Can you assist if a legal dispute arises?+

Yes. SiteDesk can maintain and collate the critical project records required — registers, correspondence, notices, claims, programs — and we regularly work alongside construction solicitors. While we don't provide legal advice, our role is to ensure records are accurate, complete, and available when required.

Will I always deal with the same person?+

Yes. You deal directly with the Director throughout the engagement — no handovers, no re-explaining, consistent accountability.
